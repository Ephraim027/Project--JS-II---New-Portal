Villa Carlina Resort

Villa Carlina Resort is located along the well-known white sand beach in Saud Beach, Pagudpud, Ilocos Norte, Philippines.
It has an accommodation built in concrete building painted with white color, NIPA roof to match the tropical surrounding nature. Its fixtures and furniture are made of elegant design wood, vinyl flooring and brick corridor.
